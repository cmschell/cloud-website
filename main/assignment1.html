Bio

My name is Christina Schell and I started working with the interwebs in 2013 as a front end web developer at Bridgepoint Education. My primary focus there was to customize the company's several microsites built in WordPress as well as maintain and update Javascript on their primary site, www.ashford.edu. I worked at the company for about 10 months before being deployed with the military for a short time in October 2014. Upon my return in February 2015, I started working at Tealium and eventually moving on to another company after my deployment.

Tealium is a Software as a Service (SaaS) company that provides Tag Management and cloud based data collection and storage. I started working as a Product Support Engineer level two where I worked with their main product, Tealium IQ. Over the past two years I have become the Senior Mobile Support Engineer and am the primary point of contact for all support related to Tealium's mobile Software Development Kit, which offers a cloud based delivery solution. I assist clients with implementing the SDK for iOS, Android and Windows platforms along with customizing their tag management solution to fit their digital strategy and data collection needs.

I separated from the military in August 2014 after 4 years active duty and 3 years in the Reserves as an officer. I am pursuing my Master's in Computer Science and Software Engineering at National University with a projected graduation date of June 2017.
